Pleasure to meet you —

My name is Garrett George.

I am a full-stack developer and music composer who loves to bring people joy by solving problems using software. I care about clean code, beautiful user interfaces, and robust data modeling, and while I feel that inner thrill every time I run a test suite and see green, I take the time to refactor afterwards. My background is in music composition, where I learned to build large projects (e.g. 20 minute work for 65-piece orchestra) that not only achieve technical rigor but touch the human spirit.

My freelance experience includes building an inventory & customer management solution for a jewelry business as well as marketing websites for a range of clients. Working with clients directly has taught me to focus on solving business problems.

I delight in working collaboratively to solve problems. My background in music ensembles has led me to cherish the dynamic energy of a collaborative environment. I am at my best on a good team, where I can bring out the best in my colleagues and vice versa.

I like Robert Glasper, good coffee, reading aloud with friends, and playing tug with my dog, Pippin, who lives with my wife Christie and me in Cincinnati, OH.

If you would like to chat or are interested in working together, please get in touch!
